Entrepreneurship Project - Intro to Engineering Design

May 2017

The objective of this project was to find a real-world problem and assess possible solutions and prior solutions to this problem.

Specifications included a real-world problem that you can solve within the two-week time limit, and if possible create a prototype or model.

This was a group project which involved lots of communication and brainstorming to find a problem to fix. Our problem was to solve lost suitcases and luggage at airports around the world. We assessed this problem and came up with a traceable suitcase with a built in GPS and proximity sensor.

Learned how to work together to sell an idea and convince the viewer why our product is better.

Image Manipulation Project - Computer Science Principles

March 2019

The objective of this project was to design a program that would manipulate images to suit a specific client.

Specifications included creating a model that was reusable for multiple images, which consisted of a border, shading, and the addition of a logo.

Learned how to ask questions for help and problem solve with a partner when you are lost on what to do.
