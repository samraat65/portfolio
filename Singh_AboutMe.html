About Me

Samraat Singh

“Hello! My name is Samraat Singh and I am currently a junior at Dublin High School. I have an interest in both STEM and business/finance, however I am still undecided in what career path I want to pursue. In order to further explore these interests I have taken multiple engineering courses and plan to take a business class next year. Outside of school, I love working on my car and also trade stocks with my father. After completing high school I wish to attend a four-year college to earn a bachelor’s degree. After that I may attend grad school as well.

I am a very reserved person, I bring a calm and mellow attitude towards most situations and am rarely stressed. This allows me to look at problems with a clear head and consider possible solutions. Take for example the time I broke my iPhone 5s. It was the third time in less than 6 months I knew my parents would be furious, but after the first 5 minutes of panic I realized freaking out would solve nothing and immediately went at solving the problem. I remembered we still had my mom’s old iPhone that was internally damaged that could be used to fix my own screen. Using the internet, I spent the next two hours transplanting my mom’s screen in replacement of my shattered one. Thinking under pressure and stress is one of my most valuable skills that I can apply anywhere in my life.

In high school I have maintained a cumulative 4.09 GPA, while competing in track and field the last two years. This year I am enrolled in Advanced Placement Calculus, Advanced Placement Chemistry, and Computer Science Principles; previously having completed courses in Principles of Engineering, Introduction to Engineering, and Biology. I am in the school orchestra, meeting everyday an hour before school to practice. I also like to work out and push my body every time I am in the gym. I know when I could have worked harder and like using that same mindset in the rest of my life in order to motivate me to achieve more.

In other words, I am a very dedicated individual who is well rounded and wants the best for himself and those around him. I want to thank you for visiting my site and I invite you to explore my resume and projects, Thank you.
