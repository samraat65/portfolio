About Me

Samraat Singh

“Scientists dream about doing great things. Engineers do them.” - James Michener. Ever since I was young both my parents and I knew I wanted to apply myself to something engineering related. As a sophomore in high school. I am not yet certain what career path I want to take but I do plan on completing both high school and get a degree in a 4-year college, likely with an engineering major. I like to tinker, I like to toy around with things until they break: pushing it to its limits till it can’t deal with it anymore, and then I rebuild it. Any lego invention I ever spent hours building ended up only to be desecrated within seconds of its birth.

If I am interested in something I stay determined or stay stubborn depending on how you look at it. Whenever I am handed a problem and see that I am capable of it I will try everything I know and when that doesn’t work I go to my best friend, Google. From there I can spend hours of time finding out the fastest and easiest way to do something. Take for example the time when I broke my iPhone. I had panicked but after I got after the sudden urge to be scared I got my wits together and went on the internet, after about 30 minutes I thought about what I had at home that I could use. I found my mom’s old broken iPhone (The phone hardware inside was broken, not the screen) and over the next 2 hours I replaced my screen using my mom’s old phone’s screen and a 20-minute youtube video. Thinking under pressure in this situation led me to discover and figure out something I had no understanding of prior,

Along with being academically proficient, I currently throw shot put and discus in track and field. We practice everyday and learn to be patient and meticulous in technique. Other skills I have are: basic programming skills in Ruby and Python, along with a complete aptitude of Autodesk Inventor. I am currently in Principles of Engineering, Advanced Placement Statistics, and Honors Chemistry, where I challenge myself everyday.

In other words, I connect with anything that is hands on and gets you engaged. I want to thank you for visiting my site and I invite you to explore the rest of my projects and resume. Thank you.
